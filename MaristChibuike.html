About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio! I'm Marist Chibuike, a passionate data scientist and statistician with a strong drive for working with data to create meaningful solutions.

With over 4 years of experience in the field, I have honed my skills in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning. My journey started with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Mathematics and Statistics from Enugu State University of Science and Technology, Agbani.

My expertise extends to:

My goal is to apply these skills to real-world challenges, turning data into actionable insights that drive decision-making and innovation. I'm excited to explore opportunities in data science and data engineering roles where I can make a meaningful impact.

When I'm not working with data, you can find me exploring new datasets, contributing to open-source projects on Github, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in the world of data.
