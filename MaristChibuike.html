About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio! I'm Marist Chibuike, a passionate data scientist and statistician with a strong drive for working with data to create meaningful solutions, particularly in humanitarian contexts.

With over 4 years of experience in the field, I have honed my skills in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning. My journey started with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Mathematics and Statistics from Enugu State University of Science and Technology, Agbani.

My curiosity and commitment to continuous learning led me to DataCamp, where I completed an Associate's degree in Data Science and Data Engineering. Recently, I've expanded my expertise with Google Cloud Data Analyst certification, enabling me to leverage cloud technologies for data analysis at scale.

I am passionate about using data science to address humanitarian challenges and improve lives. My combined expertise in data analytics and humanitarian work allows me to bridge the gap between technical solutions and real-world impact.
